Track your out-of-pocket spending for a week

Keep track of all the money you spend for a week on groceries, gas, meals, clothes, entertainment, personal items, and even coffee and snacks.

List your regular monthly bills

such as your mortgage or rent, car loan, utilities, phone, Internet service, cable TV, credit card bills.

Create a plan

Review all of your expenses for ways to cut back, and then decide what to do with the extra money.
